Relax

Relax automatically mutes your internal speakers and pauses music apps when you disconnect headphones.

  • Mutes internal speakers when headphones disconnect or unplug.
  • Pauses Spotify & iTunes / Apple Music automatically.
  • Works with wired and wireless headphones and speakers.

Get Relax: dangercove.com/relax

Denied

Denied saves your ears from Nickelback or any other band, song or album. It sees what you're playing in Spotify and iTunes (including Apple Music) and automatically skips songs that you dislike.

  • Blocks explicit songs.
  • Setup rules to skip artists, titles or albums.
  • Skips repeating tracks.
  • Shows Now Playing info.
  • Blocks 'disliked' songs in iTunes.

Get Relax: dangercove.com/denied
